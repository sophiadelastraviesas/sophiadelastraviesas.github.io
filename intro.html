// Biography // 

I am currently a student at Miami Arts Studio. During my free time, I enjoy capturing life's small moments and sharing them online. I have always been surrounded by technology and as technology advances, I become more interested. Photography and videography are my favorite hobbies. I enjoy editing the photos and making fun video montages of my favorite moments. I love to travel and of course take photos of what I see, as well as videos of the activities I do. I'm obsessed with all animals and plan to work with them as well.

// Goals // 

Videographer

I have always been interested in videography and cameras in general. I would love to travel to different places and record all the animals I encounter or any fun adventures I get up to. I would then edit the footage for other people to ssee as well.

Photographer

Like videography, I am very interested in Photography. Similar to videographers, I would like to take pictures of animals as well as interesting sites I've seen. I would love to take photos at special events as well.

Dog Sitter

I love dogs and would definitely love caring for and playing with them all day. I have a dog so I have experience and know how to care for them. I would also enjoy working with and caring for other animals as well.
